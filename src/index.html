Home

Nowadays, many families need such a good babysitter to take care for their children. Sometimes may be for all day or a few hours. The most important thing for the family is to know the babysitter very well, so that families can do their work and trust the babysitter. However, it's hard to find.

Tenderful is considered state-of-the-art. It helps families by allowing them to choose a trustful babysitter according to their needs based on the babysitter's information and evaluation. There are two benefits of applying Blockchain. The first one is to have a static and transparent platform which records babysitter 's activity history with all credibility. The main concept of the Blockchain is that its data are unmodifiable so it will never get manipulated. The second thing is because it's decentralized anyone who has experience can become a babysitter without the need to apply to any company. So, the babysitter can create her own business.
